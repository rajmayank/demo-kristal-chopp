The municipal election of 2022 is finally underway. But - if truth be told - the mayoralty of Norfolk has been contested around the council table for the past three years. A vote for Kristal Chopp is a vote to end the gamesmanship and politicking as well as a reminder to council members to put the best interests of Norfolk County in the forefront and keep them there! Four years ago, Norfolk was rudderless and headed for the rocks. Since 2010, the County had run deficits every single year except 2012 and our reserve accounts had been completely depleted in order to maintain tax and service levels artificially. Unfortunately, the piggy bank had completely run out. I believe it will take another four years to complete a course correction and finally moor the county in the safe harbour of solvency and prudent policy with an eye to common sense and the long view. I'm a proven navigator with a firm hand on the tiller. Together, we can get Norfolk where it needs to be!
